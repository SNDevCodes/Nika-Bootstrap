Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga and anime series that follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu sorcerers to fight evil curses that threaten humanity. Along with other sorcerers, Yuji learns to control cursed energy and battles powerful curses, all while navigating complex relationships and his own internal struggles. The series features a unique blend of horror, action, and comedy, and has gained popularity for its engaging characters and intense fight scenes.
